PHILOSOPHY

We improve the quality of our members lives. By instituting SUSTAINABLE FITNESS that doesn't just establish physical health but also mental well-being.  In a setting that fosters trust and organic growth, each member is given the tools to make long term change in you and allow you to make an impact and change amoungst others.


Our philosophy is functional, Primal, unique and most of all sustainable fitness  

we don't teach you to rely on machines , other gyms train to your comfort level, they promise you big gains from either dangerous or monotonous regimes that lead to the same results. Either you hurting yourself or you not losing weight

We provide a DYNAMIC NO-NONSENSE training regimen that allows for carrying groceries to become effortless, Yet still gives you an edge when competing in the severest obstacle courses and marathons. Each member is given the tools to make sustainable and impact change. We work daily to provide clear direction and realistic feedback while accounting for each member’s individual goals and abilities.

    ASSESS


    Races, Marathons, Obstacle courses

    We seek to improve the quality of our members lives. We get to know you, find out about your prior workout history, injuries and any sourness. Allowing us to develop a sustainable diet and regimen that suites you. We work with you to set realistic goals. Then we introduce you to all our members who have also made similar goals

    Cardio, Strength-training, Diet
